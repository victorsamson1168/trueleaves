Our Mission

Our mission is to grow and provide sustainable, safe and healthy food to everyone at affordable prices all year round. We aspire achieve our mission by growing food locally, using responsible and innovative farming methods that maximise yields, optimize natural resources and are profitable

1

Reduced Food Miles

By growing locally we reduce the Farm-To-Table distance from miles to food steps. Food grown locally , closer to the point of consumption becomes independent of food supply chain.

2

Fresh Harvest

Our hydroponic farm systems produce delicious and healthy greens and microgreens that are 100% GMO-free, free of pesticides and chemicals and full of flavours.

3

Belongingness

Our farm is the perfect place to gain deeper understanding about the food that you buy from us. Additionally we offer you a chance to see a farm in action and experience future of farming.

4

Sustainability

While caring about our people, we are fully aware of our footprints on the Planet. We are gentle and responsible in our farming methods as we Grow Sustainability.

5

Food Security

Everyone has a right to enjoy healthy and safe food. We offer or produce at affordable prices so that everyone has access to locally grown food.

What We Grow

Super Greens: We cultivate leafy greens and microgreens and lovingly cal them as “super greens” as they are

•••

Super Fresh

We nurture our greens at every stage- from seedling to ! We closely monitor their growth right from seedling and feed them exactly what they need during growth. We allow the crops to grow to full-size while still remaining tender and flavourful.

•••

Super Pure

Our greens and microgreens are of the highest quality and grown purely, without using pesticides, herbicides, fungicides, or any other chemicals. This makes our produce nutritionally dense, healthier, safe and protected.

•••

Super Clean

Our greens are clean right from seedling up to your kitchen. Most greens are washed in chemicals for preservation because they are grown outdoors amongst pollutants and are transported thousands of miles. We grow our products in greenhouses that are climate-controlled and clean and use only the best practices to maintain their cleanliness. This ensures that our produce is always fresh while reducing considerable travel time, distribution and fuel costs.

•••

Superlight on Footprints

We call our produce “green” because our approach right from seed to packaging is “green”. Our enclosed farming areas ensure optimum growing conditions like perfect temperature, humidity and natural sunlight for plants to flourish in all seasons. This means we can provide quality food anytime and anywhere. Our produce is always fresh as we do not harvest unless you place orders. We deliver within 3 hours of placing orders. No post- harvest treatment/cold storage is required.
