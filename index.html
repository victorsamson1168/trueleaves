Who we are

We are Ponic Farms LLP and We grow flavours, nutrition and sustainability! We are here to bridge the gap between people and nutritious produce by making it affordable for everyone. We strive to offer food security for our community and, to us, this means maintaining a steady supply of clean foods that are free of pesticides, herbicides, and fungicides. Our farming methods are environment-friendly, using at least 80% less water than land and traditional agriculture practices. We harvest our vegetables and mushrooms only when ordered, ensuring freshness upon delivery.

Our story

When the going gets tough, the tough get Growing! Our story began as a terrace garden project during the COVID-19 pandemic when we recognized a desperate need for locally grown food that was clean and free from harmful chemicals. We focused our attention on growing delicious leafy greens and microgreens using hydroponic methods. We realised that there was more to food than just eating to nourish and energise our bodies. We were assured that our food was free from contamination- was not doused in wax or injected with steroids for premature ripening or dipped in colours to make them symmetric vegetables. Having refined our processes, we’re now ready to scale up to commercial production so that everyone can enjoy our bountiful harvest.

Grow Sustainability

We are gentle and responsible in our farming methods as we Grow Sustainability. Our farming methods allow us to minimize reliance on natural resources ( arable land and water) and maximize yields. Our produce is always:


  • Pesticide Free: Our greens and microgreens are of the highest quality and grown purely, without using pesticides, herbicides, fungicides, or any other chemicals. This makes our produce nutritionally dense, healthier, safe and protected.

  • Clean: Our greens are clean right from seedling up to your kitchen. Most greens are washed in chemicals for preservation because they are grown outdoors amongst pollutants and are transported thousands of miles. We use eco-friendly practices to maintain cleanliness at our farms.

  • 85% less water and land: Our advanced hydroponic methods conserve 85% less water and land as compared to conventional farming. That means we are more environmentally sustainable and we grow more for less.

  • Green: We call our produce “green” because our approach right from seed to packaging is “green”. Our We grow in greenhouses that ensure optimum growing conditions like perfect temperature, humidity and natural sunlight for plants to flourish in all seasons. We generate minimal food and resources waste and ensure that our packaging practices are kind.
